Analysis to Support Gang Enforcement

This presentation outlines how the Wilmington, NC Police Department used data analysis and interagency collaboration to combat a surge in gang-related violence beginning around 2012. It highlights the critical role of intelligence-led policing, community engagement, and strategic enforcement in restoring public safety.
